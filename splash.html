This toxic and illegal practice is going on all across the city. Join us in asking the BBMP to crack down on open garbage burning.

Health officials estimate that as many as 40% of Indian children suffer from chronic resperatory disease due to air pollution, which shortens lives, limits opportunities, and creates huge public health costs for us all.

But we can do something about it. Open air garbage burning is one of the leading causes of air pollution, and it's already illegal -- now we need the BBMP to crack down on its own contractors who are some of the biggest culprits.
